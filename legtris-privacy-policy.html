Privacy Policy

Legtris  ·  Effective date: May 2025
This policy explains how Legtris ("the app", "we", "us") handles your data. We believe in keeping things simple and transparent. The app uses Google AdMob to serve ads, which means some data is collected automatically — this document tells you exactly what and why.

Who We Are

Legtris is an Android game developed by Radu Arsici. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at the contact details at the bottom of this page.

Data We Collect

Legtris itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. However, our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob, automatically collects the following data when the app is in use:

Device identifiers
Android Advertising ID, device model, OS version
Network information
IP address, mobile carrier, connection type
Location
Approximate location derived from IP address
Usage & diagnostics
Ad interactions, app performance data

All data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using TLS.

How Data Is Used

The data collected by AdMob is used for:

  • Serving relevant advertisements within the app
  • Measuring ad performance and effectiveness
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Analytics to improve ad delivery

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for any purpose outside of what is described above.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

Google may use the collected data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review it:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google also provides information on how they use data from partner sites and apps:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Opting Out of Personalised Ads

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your Android device settings:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • Tap "Delete advertising ID" or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

You can also visit adssettings.google.com to manage your Google ad preferences.

Children's Privacy

Legtris is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

The app uses standard (non-child-directed) AdMob ad serving. If you believe the app is being used by children, please contact us.

Data Retention

Legtris does not store any user data on our own servers. Any data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's own data retention policies, available at Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. by opting out of ad personalisation)

For data held by Google, please use Google's account tools or refer to their privacy policy. For any other enquiries, contact us directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
