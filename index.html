Legtris

Experience a revolutionary twist on the classic block puzzle! Legtris combines the timeless gameplay of clearing lines with intense, strategic boss battles that will test your reflexes and planning like never before. CLASSIC PUZZLE, EPIC CHALLENGES Stack tetrominoes, clear lines, and rack up high scores. But stay alert—the game changes when a Boss appears! These massive entities disrupt your board, spawn special bricks, and require clever strategies to defeat. Clear the boss-specific bricks to emerge victorious and advance to the next level. BATTLE UNIQUE BOSSES The journey is paved with powerful foes. Every few levels, a new boss challenges your supremacy. From shifting patterns to indestructible obstacles, each boss brings a unique mechanic to the board. Can you handle the pressure when the screen starts filling up? MULTIPLE GAME MODES • Normal Mode: The definitive experience. Rise through the levels, defeat the gauntlet of bosses, and reach the final showdown. • Hardcore Mode: For those who crave speed. Start at maximum difficulty with darker visuals and more aggressive gameplay. One mistake could be your last! • Infinite Mode: Think you're the best? After defeating the final boss in Hardcore, enter Infinite Mode where the speed never stops increasing. How long can you survive? KEY FEATURES: • Dynamic Combo System: Chain line clears for massive score multipliers and legendary voice cues like "Fantastic!" and "Crazy!" • Tactical "Hold" Mechanic: Save that crucial piece for the perfect moment to turn the tide of a boss battle. • Hall of Fame: Secure your place among the legends. Track your high scores and compete for the top spot. • Token Store & Continues: Earn tokens through gameplay milestones and new records. Use them for strategic continues to keep your epic run alive. • Immersive Audio: Satisfying sound effects and energetic voice lines that cheer you on as you dominate the board. MASTER THE BRICKS Whether you’re a casual player looking for a fun puzzle or a hardcore veteran seeking the ultimate test of skill, Legtris offers a fresh, exciting, and challenging take on the block-stacking genre. Download Legtris today and see if you have what it takes to defeat the bosses and conquer the board!
